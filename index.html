Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers for our events, educational programs, and administrative support. Please fill out our volunteer application form on our website or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@omooduduwa.org

Do you offer internships?

Yes! We offer internships in various departments including arts programming, education, preservation, and administration. Check our careers page for current opportunities.

Can I donate artifacts or artworks?

We are honored when individuals wish to donate cultural items to our collection. Please contact our curatorial team at collections@omooduwa.org to discuss potential donations.

How can I bring your programs to my community?

We love partnering with communities! Please contact our community engagement team at community@omooduduwa.org to discuss possibilities for collaboration.

Cultural Preservation

Safeguarding the rich heritage of Yoruba culture for future generations through documentation, conservation, and community engagement.

Our Preservation Mission

We believe that cultural heritage is the foundation of identity and community. Our preservation efforts focus on documenting oral traditions, protecting sacred sites, and ensuring that traditional practices continue to thrive in modern contexts.

  • •Oral history documentation with community elders
  • •Digital archiving of cultural artifacts and practices
  • •Traditional craft and skill preservation workshops
  • •Sacred site protection and restoration projects

Get Involved in Preservation

Join our preservation efforts and help safeguard Yoruba cultural heritage for future generations.
